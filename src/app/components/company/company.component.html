Who we supply

We supply components to a mix of OEM's and Tier 1 companies globally across a wide range of industries including; light vehicle, heavy vehicle, health & home, energy, tech & infrastructure, general industrial and distributors.

Our services

We deliver comprehensive support to our customers across every requirement, from concept design through to technical engineering consultancy, manufacturing, supply management and global logistics

Global footprint

The TR Group of companies consists of 33 locations within the UK, Asia, Europe and the USA including seven high volume sites manufacturing cold forged fasteners and special parts and three technical and innovation centres.
